Software Development

Over the last year, Tim developed and iOS app with the mission to revolutionize the way students engage with one another on a college campus. Every day each student receives information from the school and from one another via multiple different platforms. Whether that's an email about a school event, a GroupMe message from your dorm RA, a facebook post about housing, textbooks being sold on instagram, sports news on Twitter, text messages, canvas notifications... the list goes on and on!

Introducing CampusPost -- an exclusive platform for students to share, connect, and exchange on a college campus.

News

After Tim's spring semester abroad in Spain was canceled due to COVID-19, he and a few of his friends decided to relocate for the semester and take advantage of online classes. As someone who is immunocompromised, Tim decided that it would be safer to escape the crowded and reckless BC off-campus neighborhood and find a place in the U.S. where he could avoid the virus and recreate his anticipated adventures abroad. As a result, Tim is currently taking classes in Oahu, Hawaii!
