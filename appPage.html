Tim and his brother Matt came up with the idea for CampusPost in January of 2020. Originating as only a marketplace, the app developed into a complex platform for student posts, school events, and direct messaging. Without any previous knowledge of app development or design, Tim read extensively about SwiftUI and explored various resources on the internet. Using Firebase as a backend server, CampusPost is now live at several schools across the Northeast!
