As a kid Tim loved flying remote control helicopers and taking photos. As drone technology advanced over the last 5 years, Tim decided to purchase a video drone and combine his interests. With extra time during quarantine and over the summer, Tim recorded clips of boats and scenery around his lake house in New Hampshire. Combining the clips into an iMovie project, he recently published the movie to YouTube.
