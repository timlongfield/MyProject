As a kid, Tim loved flying remote control helicopers and taking photos. Over the last few years, drone technology has advanced tremendously, and this summer Tim decided to purchase DJI's Mavic Air 2 drone. With extra time during quarantine and over the summer, Tim recorded clips of boats and scenery around his lake house in New Hampshire. Combining the clips into an iMovie project, he recently published the video to YouTube.
