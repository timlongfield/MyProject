News

BOSTON → HAWAII

After Tim's spring semester abroad in Spain was canceled due to COVID-19, he and a few of his friends decided to relocate for the spring semester and take advantage of online classes. As someone who is immunocompromised, Tim decided that it would be safer to escape the crowded and reckless BC off-campus neighborhood and find a place in the U.S. where he could avoid the virus and recreate his anticipated adventures abroad. As a result, Tim is currently taking classes in Oahu, Hawaii!
